     i.Fest is an idea conceptualized by the IEEE Student Branch at DA-IICT. Our main aim is to bring the budding engineers from all over India to a common platform so that they can share their knowledge with fellow imaginers. "i.Fest'13 Connected Thinking" shall create magic by bringing the right mix of minds, perspectives and ambition with the fusion of technology and fun. Connected Thinking is about stretching ourselves to bring new perspectives that challenge conventional thoughts and solutions. It's about bringing best of ideas and technologies together. i.Fest'13 will have refreshing challenges, inspiring interactions and ingenious concepts and most importantly, fun 'n' frolic.

Established in Gandhinagar, Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology represents a perfect model for Wave - 4 educational innovation in Gujarat. With its primary focus on higher education through maximum participation from technology itself, DA-IICT has maintained its reputation of being one of the best engineering universities of India. DA-IICT, promoted by Dhirubhai Ambani Foundation and Anil Dhirubhai Ambani Group is a private university, included in the list of the universities maintained by University Grants Commission (UGC). DA-IICT was started with a vision to provide India with a research oriented institute that matches international standards and produces pioneers in the field of Information and Communication Technology. From its very inception, the university has been a centre for revolutionary research and innovations and a juncture for some of the greatest minds to collaborate in their quest for advancement of science and technology.
With a functional architecture, surrounded by fascinating flora and fauna, environment friendly design and a landscape planned in such a way that it conserves excess of natural resources - the campus itself is an example of what it wants to achieve - innovations through engineering.

The Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers Student Branch at DA-IICT endeavors to promote awareness among students on the opportunities that exist in the field of ICT . Our objective is to bridge the gap between industry and students studying in technology-related fields, while promoting and providing an intellectually stimulating university environment for the student body.
IEEE Student Branch, DA-IICT aims to provide an interactive platform for students to develop professional and technical abilities. Since the inception, it is successful in organizing various events like workshops , seminars , conferences with well defined objectives to encourage youth for technological enhancements and hope to continue the same. IEEE student branch daiict has been winning the best student branch award in Gujarat section since past 2 years .

  • i.vision

    How many times in your life have you stumbled upon an idea in your mind, but haven’t had the will to realize and express it? If yes, then you finally have a place where you can identify and express this vision. The ideas that you have hidden in your mind for so long, will finally find its audience. There are three different topics and the idea can be of anything which promises to solve problems faced by society or which has the potential to bring in positive change. Find innovative ways to connect how you view technology and how it can be integrated to benefit society, and you will find yourself appreciated in more ways than one!
    If you think you live in a perfect world, CONNECT your THINKING in a way useful to the society!

    WHAT’S UP FOR GRABS?

    - The top ideas of iVision (selected for phase 2) stand a chance to be incubated i.e the ideas will be financially funded, mentored by experts and patented in the students' names so that they can materialize.
    - Cash prizes and goodies

    EVENT STRUCTURE:
    The event will be conducted in 2 phases.

    Phase 1:
    Students will have to submit an abstract for their idea. The abstract should give
    1) an over view of the idea,
    2) how will it impact society,
    3) what resources it will require
    4) who will benefit from it

    The abstract has to be a Microsoft Word document or any other text file (Notepad, WordPad, pdf). It should also contain the names contact details of the participants. The abstract should be sent via email.
    Email address: ideas.ivision2013@gmail.com

    Phase 2:
    Short-listed ideas from Phase 1 will have to be presented at DA-IICT during iFest 2013 on 26th Oct. The presentation should be a detailed and enhanced version of the abstract. Details regarding how the idea is supposed to be implemented will have to be provided in the presentation. Participants will be judged by experts from the academia and industry.

    RULES AND REGULATIONS:
    • The entry can be from an individual or a group of maximum 3 people.
    • Multiple entries by the same person or group in a particular topic will not be allowed.
    • Each person or group can give only a single entry each topic.
    • The ideas have to be original. Plagiarism of any form will not be tolerated. Participants doing so will be disqualified from the event. Ideas selected will be strictly scrutinized for plagiarism.
    • Try to mention the sources for all the facts and data involved.
    This is your chance to be the innovators of tomorrow, today!
